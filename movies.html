Black Television and Film
The list includes a short description of the tv show or movie! You can watch most of these on Netflix, Hulu or other streaming services.
Name Genre Description
Moonlight Coming of Age Drama Tells the story of a Black man through three stages in his life: youth, teenage and adulthood as he struggles with his sexuality and identity
Creed Sports Drama Spin-off of the Rocky movie series starring Michael B. Jordan as Apollo Creed's son who strives to have his own boxing career like his father
If Beale Street Could Talk Romantic Drama Tells the story of a young Black woman seeking to clear the name of her wrongfully incarcerated lover in the early 1970s
Black-ish Comedy Follows the life of an upper middle class Black family residing in California as they deal with sociopolitical and personal problems
Insecure Comedy Drama Created by Issa Rae and tells the story of the awkward experience of a Black Women as they navigate through relationships, career and other drama in Los Angeles
When They See Us Crime Tragedy The true and painful story that explores the lives of five Black children involved in the Central Park jogger case
Black Panther Superhero Action Based on the Marvel Comics of the same name. T'Challa deals with being the king of Wakanda after the tragic death of his father as he tries to protect his people while facing threats from multiple enemies
She's Gotta Have It Romantic Comedy Nola Darling is a young black woman who lives in New York who struggles with balancing her relationships with different lovers and being true to herself.
Dear White People Comedy Drama Focusing on the lives of Black students who attend a predominantly white Ivy League College and all the social injustice, discrimination and political problems they face.
Juice Crime Thriller Follows the daily lives of four teens from Harlem as they deal with police, violence and growing up all at the same time
Poetic Justice Romantic Drama Starring Janet Jackson as Justice who is a writer and recites poems throughout the movie while she gets caught up in a trip to Oakland
